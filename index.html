About Cedar

We provide standard paediatric care including neonatal clinic, child development clinic, nutritional clinic immunization services etc. We understand that paediatric care means more than just treating a sick child, it involves providing parents with the necessary information needed through out their child's growth. We have skilled paediatricians who are committed to providing the best care giving you information you need to make treatment decision.
